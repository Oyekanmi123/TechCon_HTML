Our History

TechCon began in 2010 with the vision of bringing together technology enthusiasts, innovators, and industry leaders to share ideas and collaborate on groundbreaking projects. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most anticipated tech events worldwide.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to inspire and empower individuals and organizations through technology. We aim to foster innovation, encourage collaboration, and create opportunities for lifelong learning.

Notable Past Speakers

  • Dr. Ada Nduka Oyom: A renowned advocate for women in tech and founder of SheCodeAfrica.

  • Elon Musk: CEO of SpaceX and Tesla, sharing insights on sustainable technology and innovation.

  • Grace Hopper: A trailblazer in computer science and inventor of the first compiler.
